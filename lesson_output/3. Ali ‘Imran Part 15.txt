Have you ever seen a strong tree in a storm?
The wind blows and rain falls, but the tree stands firm because its roots are deep.
Faith is like that tree. When life’s storms come, Allah teaches us to stay firm, trust His plan, and remain patient.
After the pain of Uhud, when many Muslims were wounded, Allah ﷻ reminded them that every test carries divine wisdom. According to <i>Ibn Kathīr</i>, Allah decrees hardships to purify the believers’ hearts and make their faith stronger. <i>Imam al-Rāzī</i> explains that through difficulties, Allah reveals who truly believes and who wavers.
Similarly, <i>Maʿārif al-Qurʾān</i> teaches that even mistakes and setbacks can draw believers closer to Allah when they respond with repentance and patience.
Loss and pain are not signs of rejection but part of Allah’s mercy to help us grow. These verses remind us that faith is tested, purified, and strengthened through life’s changing conditions.
