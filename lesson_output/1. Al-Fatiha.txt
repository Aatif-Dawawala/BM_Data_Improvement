Surah Al-Fatiha, also known as 'The Opening', is the first chapter of the Quran and was revealed in Mecca. It's a prayer that Muslims recite in every unit of their prayers, asking Allah for guidance, mercy, and help.
This Surah was revealed to the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) as a form of guidance, not just for him but for all of humanity. It sets the foundation for the rest of the Quran, summarizing the essence of the Quran's message in just seven verses. It's a conversation between us and Allah, showing us how to praise Him, ask for help, and seek guidance on the straight path.
