When the Prophet ﷺ led the believers to Uḥud, their faith was tested. Allah allowed these moments so that true trust in Him would shine through. Tests are not to break us but to reveal what is within our hearts, guiding us to greater sincerity and steadfastness in faith.
When Prophet Muhammad ﷺ set out early from Madinah for the Battle of Uḥud, he carefully placed the believers in their positions. He reminded them that unity and obedience bring strength. However, some companions left their post seeking spoils, which caused confusion.
Imam al-Rāzī explains that this event revealed the difference between those who sought Allah’s pleasure and those driven by worldly reward. Allah allowed this setback so believers could understand that true victory comes only through steadfast faith and obedience, not numbers or strategy.
According to <i>Maʿārif al-Qurʾān</i>, even the angels’ help was by Allah’s command, teaching that success always depends on obedience and trust in Him.
