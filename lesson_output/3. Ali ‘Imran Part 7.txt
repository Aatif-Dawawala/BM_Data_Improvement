Have you ever stood firm in truth while others doubted you?
 These verses take us to that moment, when the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ met the Christians of Najrān in a test of faith.
Here, conviction defeated confusion, and Allah’s truth shone brighter than any argument or worldly power.
In these verses, Allah told the Prophet ﷺ to invite the Christians of Najrān to <i>Mubāhalah</i> - a special prayer asking Allah to curse whoever is lying (<i>Ibn Kathīr</i>). The Prophet came with his beloved family - ʿAlī, Fāṭimah, al-Ḥasan, and al-Ḥusayn  showing his full trust in Allah. When the Christians saw his sincerity, they realized the truth and stepped back from the challenge.
Allah reminded them that just as Ādam was created from dust, ʿĪsā (AS) was also created by Allah’s command, not by human means. The Qur’an then calls everyone to a <i>common word</i>- to worship only Allah. This story teaches us that true faith stands firm without anger, pride, or argument.
