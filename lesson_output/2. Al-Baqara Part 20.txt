These verses teach the rules that guide family life in Islam. Allah gives clear limits for marriage, divorce and care within the home. Through this guidance we learn how faith shapes responsibility and protects dignity in moments that test the heart.
In these verses Allah explains the rulings of divorce, reconciliation and the care of women and children. Scholars like Ibn Kathir explain that Allah commands believers to keep marriage with fairness or release with goodness. This teaches that separation must follow His limits with respect. The mention of khul, as described by classical scholars, shows that a woman may seek release when living together becomes harmful. 
These verses also protect widows and divorced women by giving them provision and time before new decisions. Through these rulings the Quran shows that family ties must follow justice, mercy and awareness of Allah.
