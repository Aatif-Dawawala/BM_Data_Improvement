Love is more than words, it's what the heart does when no one sees. 
True love for Allah is shown through following His Messenger ﷺ and trusting His wisdom. 
When faith becomes obedience, it turns into living love, a love that changes hearts, brings peace, and draws Allah’s mercy.
Allah begins with a call that touches every heart:
“Say, [O Muhammad], ‘If you love Allah, then follow me; Allah will love you and forgive your sins.’” (Al ‘Imran 3:31)
Tafsir Ibn Kathir explains that this verse separates true love from empty claims whoever truly loves Allah follows the Prophet ﷺ. Ibn Juzzay adds that obedience is the door to divine love.
Then come stories of pure devotion: Ḥannah’s vow to dedicate her child to Allah, Maryam’s miraculous provision in her prayer room), and Zakariyyā’s heartfelt du‘aa’ for a child. 
Each story teaches that sincere faith and patient trust make hearts fertile for miracles for when love is true, Allah’s mercy responds.
