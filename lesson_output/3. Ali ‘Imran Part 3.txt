Have you ever watched the sun rise after a long dark night?
 Just as light always returns, Allah’s justice always shines, even when hidden for a while. 
No wrong escapes His sight, and no act of goodness is ever forgotten. 
Allah’s timing is perfect, His justice never fails.
These verses show that Allah’s justice is complete and His power absolute. He warns those who harm the believers or reject His messages that no strength can save them. As <i>Ibn Kathīr</i> explains, Allah raises the truthful, even ordinary people to the rank of prophets through their sincerity.
Some, as <i>Al-Rāzī</i> notes, falsely believed punishment would touch them only briefly. But Allah’s justice is never partial or delayed. During the Battle of the Trench, when hypocrites mocked the Prophet ﷺ’s hope in Allah’s help, Allah revealed the powerful prayer: <i>“O Allah, Owner of all kingdoms.”</i> 
It reminds us that all honor, victory, and goodness belong to Him alone - <i>“In Your hand is all good.”</i>
