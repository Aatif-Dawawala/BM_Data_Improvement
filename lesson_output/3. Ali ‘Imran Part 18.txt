Have you ever felt afraid but still said, “Allah is enough for me”?
These verses celebrate that courage. When fear surrounded the believers after Uhud, they placed their full trust in Allah.
True strength is not the absence of fear; it is faith that overcomes it through trust in Him.
After Uhud, rumors spread that the enemies were preparing to attack again. Some were afraid, but the true believers said, <i>“Allah is enough for us, and He is the best protector.”</i>
According to <i>Ibn Kathīr</i>, their words showed complete trust, turning fear into peace. This was a moment of divine testing when sincere hearts drew nearer to Allah. <i>Maʿārif al-Qurʾān</i> explains that Allah rewarded this trust with calm, safety, and new courage.
These verses show that faith does not remove hardship; it changes how we face it. When we rely on Allah alone, fear becomes strength, and trials become steps toward peace and spiritual success.
