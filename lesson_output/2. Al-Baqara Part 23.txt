Ayat al-Kursi, the Throne Verse, is the greatest verse in the Qur’an. It tells us about Allah’s power, protection, and care. When we know that the One who never sleeps watches over us, our hearts feel safe and full of peace.
This verse is called <i>Ayat al-Kursi</i>, meaning <i>The Throne Verse</i>. Prophet Muhammad ﷺ said it is the greatest verse in the Qur’an (<i>Ibn Kathir</i>). It tells us about Allah’s most perfect qualities. He is <i>Al-Hayy</i>, The Ever-Living, and <i>Al-Qayyum</i>, The Sustainer of all things. Allah never gets tired or sleepy. His <i>Kursi</i> or seat of authority is so vast that it covers the heavens and the earth.
Some scholars said it shows His knowledge and power (<i>Ibn Juzay, Al-Razi</i>). When we recite this verse, Allah protects us from harm, and the Prophet ﷺ said that nothing prevents the one who recites it after every fard prayer from entering Paradise except death (Ma'arif al-Qur'an).
