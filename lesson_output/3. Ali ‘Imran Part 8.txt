Have you ever seen clear water turn muddy when mixed with dirt? 
Truth is the same, pure and bright until someone stirs in falsehood. 
In these verses, Allah warns against twisting truth for selfish gain. Only hearts that stay sincere and honest can see through deception and stay firm in faith.
In these verses, Allah exposes the deceit of some among the People of the Book (<i>Ibn Kathīr</i>). They pretended to accept Islam by day and reject it by night, hoping to confuse new believers. Others changed their scripture with their tongues, hiding the truth about the Prophet ﷺ (<i>3:78</i>). 
Some even excused dishonesty in trade, though Allah praised those who were truly trustworthy, those who would return even a heap of gold without hesitation.
These verses remind us that faith is not just belief, but character. Lying, cheating, or twisting words weakens the heart. 
Truthfulness brings light and peace. Every act of honesty is a reflection of real īmān, faith that lives in action.
