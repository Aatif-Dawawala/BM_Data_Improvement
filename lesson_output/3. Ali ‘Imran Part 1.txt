Every building stands on a strong foundation and so does faith.
True belief is built upon <i>tawḥīd</i> and the guidance revealed from Allah. 
In these opening verses, Allah establishes the bedrock of all knowledge that He alone is <i>Al-Ḥayy</i> (The Ever-Living) and <i>Al-Qayyūm</i> (The Self-Sustaining).
Long ago, sixty Christian leaders from Najrān came to Madinah to meet the Prophet ﷺ. They wanted to argue about who ‘Īsā (Jesus) really was. Allah revealed these verses to remove doubt and make the truth clear. 
 The Qur’an confirms the Torah and the Gospel, completing the truth that came before. Allah alone creates life in the womb, as noted by <i>Al-Qurṭubī</i>, showing that no human can share in His power.
 The Qur’an contains <i>Muḥkamāt</i> - clear verses  and <i>Mutashābihāt</i> - hidden meanings known only to Allah. True believers stay humble, trusting Him through sincere du‘ā’.
