Have you ever given away something you truly loved, just to please Allah?
 Real faith is built on such choices not on status or words, but on sincerity and sacrifice. 
These verses remind us that faith is not inherited; it is chosen, tested, and proven by what the heart gives and holds back.
In this passage, Allah ﷻ reveals that real faith is shown through decisions of the heart. Those who reject belief cannot ransom themselves even with all the gold on earth (3:91), teaching that wealth cannot buy salvation. True goodness, or <i>al-birr</i>, is reached only when one gives up what is most loved for Allah’s sake like Abū Ṭalḥah (RA), who donated his cherished garden, Bayruḥā’, after this verse was revealed.
These verses also honor the Ka‘bah, the first house of worship built for all humankind as a place of peace marked by the footsteps of Ibrāhīm (AS) (<i>Ibn Juzayy</i>). Finally, Allah warns believers to guard their unity and avoid division.
