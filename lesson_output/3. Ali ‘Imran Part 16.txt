Do you ever worry about what will happen next success, loss, or change? These verses remind us that everything happens by Allah’s will and wisdom. When believers trust His plan, fear leaves and peace enters the heart.
True faith means knowing Allah never abandons those who rely on Him.
After the pain of Uhud, fear and doubt filled many hearts. Allah ﷻ reminded the believers that victory and defeat happen only by His command and wisdom.
According to <i>Ibn Kathīr</i>, these trials were not punishments but lessons to place complete trust in Allah, not in numbers or strategies. <i>Imam al-Rāzī</i> explains that Allah caused fear to enter the hearts of the disbelievers, showing that strength without faith brings no peace.
As <i>Maʿārif al-Qurʾān</i> notes, whoever relies on Allah gains a strength that no army can give. The Prophet ﷺ stayed calm and steadfast, showing that real faith means surrendering with peace to Allah’s perfect will.
