Not all enemies fight with weapons; some harm through envy, lies, and hidden hatred. These verses uncover the unseen dangers of hypocrisy and deception. Yet believers are reminded that no harm can reach those who place full trust in Allah. Faith and patience remain their greatest protection.
In these verses, Allah ﷻ describes the deep hostility shown by some among the People of the Book due to pride and denial of truth. Their envy led to humiliation and loss, except for those who return to Allah with sincere repentance. This teaches that inner corruption brings both moral and worldly downfall.
According to <i>Maʿārif al-Qurʾān</i>, trust in Allah protects the believer’s heart from despair. Even when the Prophet ﷺ faced hidden enemies in Madinah, his patience turned their plots into protection. <i>Tafsīr Ibn Kathīr</i> adds that steadfastness and righteousness bring divine support. Therefore, every test becomes a means to reveal who truly depends on Allah with sincerity and faith.
