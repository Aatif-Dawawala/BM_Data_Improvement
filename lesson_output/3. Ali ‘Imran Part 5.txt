When a heart sincerely seeks Allah, He answers in ways that bring peace, not noise. In these verses, Allah comforts His devoted servants with signs- Zakariyyā’s peaceful silence, Maryam’s honored selection, and the birth of ‘Īsā (AS). 
Through these stories, love turns into obedience, and faith blossoms into certainty.
Every divine sign carries wisdom shaped by the believer’s heart. When Prophet Zakariyyā (AS) asked Allah for reassurance about his promised child, Allah granted him silence not as punishment but as serenity. <i>Ibn Kathīr</i> explains that he could no longer speak of worldly matters but could still glorify Allah constantly. This sacred quiet prepared him for the joy of Yahyā’s birth.
Soon after, the angels gave Maryam glad tidings: Allah had “chosen and purified” her twice first for worship, then for the miraculous birth of ‘Īsā (AS). His miracles - healing the blind, curing the sick, and reviving the dead matched the medical knowledge of his time (<i>Ibn Kathīr</i>), proving that every divine sign perfectly meets its moment.
