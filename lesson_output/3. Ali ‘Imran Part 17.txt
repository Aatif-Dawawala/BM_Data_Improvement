Have you ever done something good but felt unseen? These verses remind us that Allah sees every sincere effort.
No act of goodness is ever lost with Him.
True success is not in being praised, but in being accepted by Allah, who values sincerity over size or fame.
After the Battle of Uhud, some believers felt disheartened and questioned why loss occurred despite their faith. Allah ﷻ revealed that every soul receives exactly what it intended, and betrayal of trust leads to loss (<i>Tafsīr Ibn Kathīr</i>). These verses teach that sincerity in action always brings reward, even when success seems hidden.
According to <i>Maʿārif al-Qurʾān</i>, those who stood firm and obeyed the Prophet ﷺ were promised joy and forgiveness. Allah wanted to show that true victory is measured by loyalty, not numbers.
When the wounded companions returned to the battlefield at Ḥamrāʾ al-Asad, their devotion proved their sincerity. Allah praised their courage and promised that sincere hearts will never lose their reward.
